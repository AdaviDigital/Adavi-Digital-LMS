# Student User Manual — Adavi Digital Institute

## Getting Started
1. Go to the homepage and click **Get Started**.
2. Fill in your name, email, and password, and choose "Learn (Student)".
3. Check your email to verify your account (once email sending is configured).

## Finding a Course
1. Click **Courses** in the top navigation.
2. Browse the catalog or use filters (category, level, price, rating).
3. Click any course card to view its full details, curriculum, and instructor.

## Enrolling
- **Free courses:** click **Enroll for Free** on the course page — you're in immediately.
- **Paid courses:** click **Buy Now**, choose a payment method (Paystack, Flutterwave, Stripe, or
  PayPal) on the checkout page, and complete payment. You're automatically enrolled once payment succeeds.

## Learning
1. From the course page, click any lesson to open the lesson player.
2. Watch the video, read the transcript, and download any attached resources.
3. Use the **AI Chat Tutor** on the right to ask questions about the lesson in real time.
4. Click **Generate Summary** for an AI-written recap if you want a quick review.
5. Click **Mark lesson complete** when you're done — your progress bar updates automatically.
6. Complete any quiz or assignment linked to the lesson.

## Quizzes & Exams
- Quizzes are auto-graded instantly for multiple-choice/true-false questions.
- Exams are timed and may be randomized; some allow retakes (check the exam details).
- Essay-type questions are graded manually by your instructor.

## Assignments
- Submit a file link, project URL, or PDF link plus an optional comment.
- Your instructor will grade and leave feedback, visible on your dashboard.

## Certificates
- Once you complete 100% of a course's lessons, a certificate is issued automatically.
- View and share it from **Dashboard → Certificates**.
- Anyone can verify a certificate's authenticity at `/verify-certificate/<certificate-number>` — no
  login required.

## Your Dashboard
Your dashboard (top navigation → **Dashboard**, once logged in) shows:
- All your enrolled courses and progress bars
- Certificates earned
- Recent notifications

## Messaging & Reviews
- Message your instructor directly from a course page for questions.
- Leave a rating and review once you're enrolled in a course.

## Support
- Use the **Contact** page or WhatsApp button in the footer for help.
- Check the **FAQ** page for common questions.
